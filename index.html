Validation

The Proof of Concept Check

Before committing to any niche, run this 4-part validation. It takes 30–60 minutes and saves months of wasted effort.

1
The Instagram Search Test

Search niche keywords on Instagram (Accounts tab). 5–10 accounts with 50k+ followers posting consistently = healthy market. Ghost town = too small or too new.

2
The Growth Rate Check

Take your top 10 accounts to Socialblade.com. You want consistent steady growth over 30–90 days. 200–500 followers/day means they cracked the algorithm — replicate it.

3
The Money Test

Browse top accounts for 48 hours. Are they promoting in Stories? Shoutout posts for coaches? Affiliate links? Visible monetisation = proof money flows.

4
The Engagement Check

Measure save and share ratios on recent posts. High saves = audience finds it useful. High shares = it moves them emotionally. Both indicate buyers.

The Core Equation

Growth is a system, not a mystery

Instagram growth comes down to exactly two things. You need people to visit your profile — that is traffic. And when they arrive, you need your profile to convert them into followers — that is conversion. Without traffic, nobody sees you. Without conversion, the traffic means nothing.

THE INSTAGRAM GROWTH EQUATION
Traffic × Conversion = Growth
If you are not growing, the answer always lies in one of these two levers.
— THE TWO GROWTH PROBLEMS
Identify yours in under a minute.
TRAFFIC PROBLEM
Your CVR is good (25%+) but your account is barely growing. Solution: drive more profile visits — reels, hashtags, engagement, collaborations.
CONVERSION PROBLEM
Your content gets views and likes but people are not following. Solution: your profile is not convincing visitors. Fix logo, bio, headline, or feed first.
Your CVR

Your Follower Conversion Rate — how to measure it

Most creators have no idea how well their profile is actually converting visitors into followers. That changes right now. Open Instagram Insights and find these two numbers.

THE FOLLOWER CVR FORMULA
(Net Follows ÷ Profile Visits) × 100 = Your CVR %
Example: (5,683 follows ÷ 13,959 profile visits) × 100 = 40.7% CVR — roughly 4 out of every 10 visitors followed.
BAD
10–15%
Profile is losing most visitors. Urgent fix needed — start with logo, bio, and feed.
GOOD
15–25%
Profile is working. Optimise to push higher — and generate more traffic.
GREAT
25–35%+
You convert strongly. Focus shifts entirely to traffic generation.
9,600
extra followers in a year by improving your CVR from 10% to 30% — from the exact same traffic. Better profile, same effort, three times the result.

Your Fastest Early Growth Tool

The HTN Strategy

HTN = Highly Targeted Niche. Your ideal follower is already on Instagram, already following accounts in your niche, already engaging with content like yours. Your job is to find her and give her a reason to notice your profile.

— THE HTN SYSTEM
Borrow attention. Build conversion.
—Find 15–25 relevant accounts. Split into Big (25k+), Medium (5–25k), Small (1–5k).
—Pick your 5 Dream Accounts — whose followers are exactly who you want.
—Turn on post notifications. Comment within the first hour — thoughtful, valuable, specific.
—Reply to pinned comments — they get disproportionate visibility.
—Visit other commenters' profiles. Leave 1–3 pieces of genuine engagement.
—Repeat 4–8 times daily, 15 minutes each session.
HTN traffic converts at 3–5× the rate of cold traffic.

A single well-executed collab can deliver thousands of new followers without spending a penny on advertising. Two formats: One-Way (host tags you) and Two-Way (Instagram's native Collab feature — both feeds, shared engagement). Always push for Two-Way.

The 4 Killers

The 4 mindset traps that kill Instagram accounts

Every account that fails falls into one or more of these traps. Name them, and they lose their power over you.

01
Perfectionism Paralysis
The enemy dressed as a virtue. While you are perfecting, you are not posting. While you are not posting, you are not growing. Good enough and posted beats perfect and draft.
02
The Comparison Spiral
Instagram is designed to show you aspirational content all day. The only useful comparison is to who you were last week. Block. Mute. Focus on your own data.
03
The Inconsistency Cycle
Five posts a week for three months beats 20 in week one and silence after. The algorithm rewards reliability over bursts. Show up even when results are slow.
04
Outcome Dependency
The first 60–90 days are universally slow. Every major account lived this phase. Trust the process — results arrive on a delay, not in real time.
Protecting Your Joy

Working with the treadmill — four practices that protect your joy

The hedonic treadmill describes our mind's tendency to return to a baseline of happiness regardless of what happens. You hit 10,000 followers, joy lasts 48 hours, then 20,000 becomes the new goal. Not a character flaw — neurological wiring.

WORKING WITH THE TREADMILL
Four practices that protect your joy.
CELEBRATE DELIBERATELY
Build a ritual for every milestone. Tell someone. Write it down. Feel it before moving on.
CHASE BALANCE, NOT METRICS
Set lifestyle goals alongside follower goals. What does a successful week feel like — not just perform like?
PROCESS OVER OUTCOME
Design your life so the daily process itself is rewarding. If the work is enjoyable, you win every day regardless of what the counter says.
DEFINE ENOUGH
Decide what enough looks like before you start. $5k/mo? 3 hours of work daily? Honour it when you reach it.
